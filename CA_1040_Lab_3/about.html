About Me

A little about myself

Student. Creator. Always learning something new.

Hi, I'm Gavin Sullivan — a college student currently studying web development and exploring what it means to build things for the internet. I'm enrolled in an introductory web development course where I'm learning the building blocks of HTML and CSS and how they come together to create real websites.

I enjoy the creative side of coding — the way you can start with a blank screen and end up with something that looks and feels intentional. There's something satisfying about getting the spacing just right or watching a hover effect work exactly the way you planned.

Outside of class, I like to spend time on things that challenge me and keep life interesting. Whether it's picking up a new hobby, exploring ideas, or just figuring out how things work — I'm always curious and ready to learn.

This portfolio is one of my first projects, and I'm excited to keep adding to it as my skills grow. Thanks for stopping by!
